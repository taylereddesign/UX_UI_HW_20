Redesign of NonProfit

"We were tasked with the redesign of a nonprofit organization of our choosing. With just three weeks to conduct research, develop, test, create, iterate and summarize our design solutions, this redesign would test our ability to work cohesively and expeditiously as a team intent on producing a design that reflected the respect we have for the work being done at ReEvolution, which we both have a great deal of admiration for."
"ReEvolution was a personally fulfilling undertaking, chosen because as individuals we are passionate about work being done to end mass incarceration and combat a system intent on sustaining cycles of surveillance, detention and harm. We felt that as a young organization, ReEvolution offered a unique voice of collectivity and affectual healing through the work they were doing."
